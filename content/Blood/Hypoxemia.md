# Hypoxemia

Original URL: https://trello.com/c/IqjBLsvZ

---

Hypoxemia is the absence of adequate oxygen in the bloodstream. Hypoxemia can quickly build up and cause the patient to spiral out of control. A patient suffering from this needs immediate medical care. [[Pneumothorax](../Lungs/Pneumothorax.md)]([Pneumothorax](../Lungs/Pneumothorax.md) "smartCard-inline") will prevent Hypoxemia from going down, proportional to its strength.
